Calzone

Savor the essence of Italy with our delectable calzone recipe, a culinary masterpiece that combines the beloved flavors of pizza in a convenient handheld form. Imagine a golden-brown crust enveloping a savory medley of marinara sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings, all tucked snugly inside. Baked to perfection, each bite offers a symphony of textures and tastes, from the crisp exterior to the lusciously melty center. Whether enjoyed as a satisfying meal on the go or a crowd-pleasing appetizer, our calzone recipe is sure to transport your taste buds to the streets of Naples. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) pizza dough, homemade or store-bought
  • 1 cup (240ml) marinara sauce
  • 1 cup (120g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (60g) sliced pepperoni or your favorite pizza toppings (optional)
  • Olive oil, for brushing
  • All-purpose flour, for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Place a baking sheet or pizza stone in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Divide the pizza dough into equal portions, depending on how many calzones you want to make. Roll out each portion of dough on a lightly floured surface into a circle or oval shape, about 1/4 inch thick.
  3. Spoon a few tablespoons of marinara sauce onto one half of each dough circle, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the marinara sauce, then add your desired toppings such as pepperoni or vegetables.
  5. Carefully fold the other half of the dough over the filling to form a half-moon shape. Press the edges firmly to seal, then crimp the edges with a fork to ensure they are tightly sealed.
  6. Brush the tops of the calzones with olive oil for a golden crust.
  7. Carefully transfer the calzones to the preheated baking sheet or pizza stone. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the calzones are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve with additional marinara sauce for dipping if desired.
