Lasagna

Craving some cheesy goodness? Try out our Lasagna recipe!

Step into our kitchen, where the tantalizing scent of homemade marinara sauce and melted cheese beckons. Today, we're delving into the timeless delight of crafting the perfect lasagna. With layers of tender pasta, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheese, this recipe promises to satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. Let's dive in and create a masterpiece of comfort and flavor together.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Onion
  • Spicy Italian sausage
  • Garlic cloves
  • Can crushed tomatoes
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce: Saute onions and garlic, add ground beef or sausage, then mix in crushed tomatoes, herbs, and seasonings. Simmer until thickened.
  2. Cook the pasta: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process.
  3. Layering: Start with a layer of sauce at the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, then ricotta cheese, more sauce, and a sprinkle of mozzarella. Repeat until the dish is filled, ending with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  4. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake in a preheated oven until heated through and bubbly. Remove the foil during the last few minutes of baking to brown the cheese.
  5. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing into squares and serving hot. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
